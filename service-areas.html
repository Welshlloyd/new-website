Dog Walker Service Areas — South Melbourne & Nearby

Solo, one-dog-only walks with GPS tracking and photo updates. We keep routes local, calm and enrichment-focused across South Melbourne and neighbouring suburbs.

Where We Walk

Our walks are proudly local to South Melbourne, with regular routes around Albert Park Lake, St Vincent Gardens, the Port Melbourne foreshore, quiet streets in Middle Park, and pocket parks across Southbank. We don’t run pack walks or shuttle dogs by car — your pup gets a calm, one-to-one outing at their pace.

Neighbourhoods We Serve

  • Albert Park — lakeside loops & leafy streets
  • Port Melbourne — foreshore & reserves
  • Middle Park — beach-adjacent paths
  • Southbank — river paths & green pockets

What You Get

  • 45-minute solo walk — never group walked
  • GPS route + photo update after every walk
  • Weekdays 7am–7pm, subject to availability
  • Ethical, low-stress handling tailored to your dog

How to Book & Membership Options

New to The Woof of Walk Street? Start with our core service: Dog Walking — South Melbourne. If you’d like a predictable weekly rhythm, consider our memberships: Starter (4 walks / month), Growth (10 walks / month) or Unlimited (weekdays). Each option includes the same solo-only care, GPS transparency and photo updates — choose the cadence that suits your pup and your schedule.

FAQs

Do you offer group walks?

No. We walk one dog at a time for safety, focus and calmer dogs.

Do you pick up by car?

No. Routes are local and on-foot — it’s less stressful and more predictable.

What updates do I receive?

After every walk you’ll receive the GPS route and a photo update straight to your phone.
