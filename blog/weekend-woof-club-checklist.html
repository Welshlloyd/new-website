Weekend Woof Club: First-Timer Checklist

Boutique, one-guest boarding in South Melbourne · What to pack & how it works

TL;DR packing list: lead + harness & ID, favourite bed/blanket, measured meals, treats, any meds, and your mobile number.

Before You Book

  • We host one guest only for calmer, safer stays.
  • Daily 45-minute solo walk included (weather-sensible).
  • Photo & notes after arrival and each day.
  • Video trial visit before the first stay to see the space and chat through routines.

What To Pack

  • Lead, harness & ID (fitted, escape-proof).
  • Comfort item: bed/blanket or favourite toy.
  • Food in labelled portions + written feeding notes (or choose our kibble & cooked chicken option).
  • Medications in original packaging with clear instructions.
  • Emergency contact (your mobile + a backup).
Tip: a worn T-shirt from home helps nervous pups settle on the first night.

Arrival Day

  • Quick handover chat (meals, walks, quirks, house rules).
  • Sniff-around, water, and short decompression walk.
  • Settling time with calm enrichment (lick mat, stuffed toy).

During The Stay

  • One-to-one care — we don’t host multiple guests.
  • Daily solo walk + garden breaks.
  • Photo updates via the app so you can relax.

Pickup & Aftercare

  • Summary of meals, toileting, sleep and walks.
  • Any notes for next time (what they loved, sensitivities).
  • Optional bath & brush add-on before pickup.

First-Timer FAQs

Do you crate?

No. Stays are home-style and crate-free unless you request a familiar crate for settling.

What if my dog is picky with food?

Bring their usual meals. If appetite dips, we can switch to our gentle kibble & cooked chicken option (noted in your portal).

Can you give medications?

Yes — please provide them in original packaging with clear instructions and timings.

How far in advance should I book?

We accept one guest only, so weekends can fill quickly. If you have dates in mind, request early in the app.
