Unlimited Membership — FAQs

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — there are no lock-ins. You can cancel before your next billing cycle.

Do skipped days roll over?

No — Unlimited covers one walk per weekday. Keeping a steady rhythm is best for most dogs.

Are walks really one-dog only?

Always. Your dog receives our full attention — never group or pack walked, and never car-ferried.

Where do you walk?

Locally around South Melbourne — common routes include Albert Park Lake, St Vincent Gardens, and nearby parks.
