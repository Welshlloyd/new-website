What’s Included

Every casual walk is a calm, one-on-one adventure designed around your dog’s rhythm — not the clock. We start right from your door and wander through South Melbourne’s favourite walking spots — whether that’s looping around Albert Park Lake, a slow stroll near the market, or a peaceful laneway sniff-sesh.

Each session runs for a full 45 minutes on foot — no car rides, no rushing, no group chaos. It’s just your pup and one dedicated walker giving them the time, attention, and calm energy they deserve.

After each walk, you’ll receive a live GPS map, a photo update, and a quick note about how your furry mate went. We use positive, force-free handling so every outing builds trust, confidence, and a happy tail wag.

  • Full 45-minute solo walk — no groups ever
  • Live GPS tracking + photo updates
  • Familiar local routes (Albert Park, South Melbourne Market, and more)
  • Positive, force-free walking style

Booking & Availability

Booking your casual dog walk in South Melbourne couldn’t be easier. Head to our Time To Pet portal, choose your time between 7:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. (Mon–Fri), and you’re all set. You’ll get instant confirmation, reminders before each session, and a GPS update straight to your phone when the walk’s underway.

We often have same-day or next-day spots available, especially in South Melbourne, Albert Park, and Port Melbourne. You can book casually whenever you need a hand, or — if you start loving the routine — switch over to one of our membership plans for better rates and guaranteed weekly walks.

Think of it as dog-walking made easy: you handle the cuddles, we handle the miles.

  • Flexible Mon–Fri bookings, 7 a.m.–7 p.m.
  • Simple online booking via Time To Pet
  • Same-day slots often available
  • Perfect for one-offs, busy days, or schedule gaps

Casual Walk FAQs

How long is a casual walk?

Each casual session is a full 45 minutes on foot, not including transport or prep.

Are casual walks one-dog-only?

Yes. We never run pack walks. Casual bookings are strictly solo for calm, focused outings.

Do you cover my area?

We service South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park and Southbank.

What proof do I get after the walk?

You’ll receive a GPS route and a photo update after every walk.
