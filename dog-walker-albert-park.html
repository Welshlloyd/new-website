Professional Dog Walker in Albert Park — Solo Only

Wide paths, water views and open sight-lines make Albert Park Lake perfect for calm, one-on-one “decompression” walks. Your dog sets the tempo; we provide structure, enrichment and predictability — never pack walks or car-ferry chaos.

  • 45-minute solo sessions (no group mixing)
  • GPS route + photo update after each walk
  • Insured, police-checked, first-aid-aware handlers

Albert Park Dog Walker — FAQs

Do you walk in the park itself?

Yes — we use the lake loop and nearby streets, tailoring the route to your dog’s comfort and energy on the day.

How will I know how it went?

You’ll receive a GPS route, a photo update, and short notes after every session.

Are walks one-dog-only?

Yes — we never mix unfamiliar dogs or run pack walks. It’s calm, one-on-one attention.
