Dog Walking South Melbourne — FAQs

Do you walk more than one dog at a time?

No — all walks are strictly solo. We never group unfamiliar dogs or use car-ferrying. This keeps arousal low and focus high.

How long is each walk?

Each session is a full 45 minutes on foot (prep/transport excluded) tailored to your dog’s routine and building location.

What updates do I get?

Time-stamped GPS route, a happy photo update, and short notes (mood, toileting, energy) after every walk.

Do you cover my street in South Melbourne?

Yes — we operate across South Melbourne and immediate surrounds. We select low-arousal loops from your building entrance.

Want to chat first?

Schedule a quick 10-minute call — perfect for reactive or senior dog questions.
