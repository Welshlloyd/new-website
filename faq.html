Solo Dog Walking — Safety, Pricing & Scheduling

Our premium solo dog walking is designed for calm handling and focused attention—perfect for anxious or sensitive dogs. We never group walk; every outing is tailored.

Do you ever walk more than one dog at the same time?

Never. We’re strictly one-dog-only to keep your dog relaxed and safe, and to ensure they get true one-on-one exercise. Group walks can be overwhelming, especially for sensitive dogs.

Learn more: Premium Solo Dog Walking

What’s included in a casual solo walk?

Each casual walk includes 45 minutes of one-on-one walking (plus transport), with GPS tracking, photo updates, and a report card delivered to your secure client portal.

When are walks available and how do I book?

Standard hours are Mon–Fri, 7:00am–7:00pm. Use the Time To Pet client portal for real-time availability and bookings.

What does a casual walk cost?

$80 per 45-minute solo walk (incl. GST). Regular walkers save with memberships.

What plans do you offer?
  • Starter — 4 solo walks/month
  • Tail Growth — 10 solo walks/month

Membership details

How does Unlimited work?

One 45-minute solo walk per weekday (Mon–Fri) for the membership month for that specific dog—ideal for daily consistency.

Can I change times or skip weeks?

You can move bookings within our change window if a slot is available; due to demand, unused walks don’t roll over.

Boutique Dog Boarding — One Guest Only

Our boarding is strictly one-guest-only, replicating a calm home routine—no kennels, no stress.

How many dogs stay at once?

Your dog is the only guest, ensuring a predictable, clean environment and one-on-one care.

Boarding overview

Where will my dog sleep?

Home-style comfort—we follow your routine (bed/sofa/crate). We can pick up familiar bedding locally before the stay.

What do you feed during a stay?

We prefer you supply food for consistency. We can provide high-quality kibble plus cooked chicken on request. List allergies in your portal profile.

Do you support local shelters?

Yes—10% of profits go to local dog charities. See our impact.
