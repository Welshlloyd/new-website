Unlimited Promo – FAQs

Who qualifies for 50% off? New Unlimited members get 50% off their first month ($750). If an existing member refers a new Unlimited member, the referrer gets 50% off one future month once the friend joins.
How do referrals work? At sign-up, the new member adds their referrer’s name or email. We credit the referrer’s account with 50% off a month after the new member completes sign-up.
Can I stack multiple referral discounts? You can earn multiple 50%-off months through multiple referrals, but only one discount applies per billing period.
Does it auto-renew? Yes. After your first month at $750, Unlimited renews at the standard monthly price of $1,500 unless you cancel before your renewal date. You can cancel anytime via your account portal or by emailing info@woofofwalkstreet.com.
