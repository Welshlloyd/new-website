Weekend Woof Club

Boutique, one-guest boarding. Calm, cozy, and fully supervised — like staying with family.

What’s included

  • One guest only (no shared bowls, no kennel chaos)
  • Daily walks and enrichment tailored to your dog
  • Photo updates and notes
  • Optional meals: high-quality kibble & cooked chicken
  • Pick-up / drop-off available by arrangement

How to book

Boarding runs Friday→Sunday or Friday→Monday on select weekends. Spots are limited.

  1. New here? Create your client account in the portal.
  2. Choose “Boarding – Weekend Woof Club” and your dates.
  3. We’ll confirm availability and the 10% charity contribution (Lost Dogs’ Home & local shelters).
