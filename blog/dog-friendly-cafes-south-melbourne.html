Dog-Friendly Cafés in South Melbourne

Locals we love for good coffee, shade, and a warm welcome for dogs.

Post-walk coffee? Yes please. Here are a few South Melbourne cafés we find consistently dog friendly — with outdoor seating, water bowls, and staff who love a hello.

What makes a café truly dog-friendly?

  • Shaded outdoor seating and space to settle
  • Clean water bowls refreshed regularly
  • Calm, low-traffic areas away from scooters and prams
  • Staff who understand dog etiquette

Quick etiquette tips

  • Keep leads short and dogs under tables or beside you
  • Bring a small mat for your dog to “park” on and relax
  • Skip busy periods if your dog is still learning to settle
