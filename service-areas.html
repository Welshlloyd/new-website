Dog Walking & Boutique Boarding — Service Areas

One-dog-only walks and one-guest weekend boarding across South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park and Southbank.

Dog Walking & Boarding in South Melbourne

Our home base. Calm, one-dog-only walks around St Vincent Gardens, the Market laneways and nearby streets. Every outing includes GPS tracking and photo updates. Weekend boarding is one guest only in a quiet, home-style setting.

See South Melbourne Walking Weekend Boarding

Dog Walking in Albert Park

Tailored routes around Albert Park Lake with clear sight-lines and space to decompress — great for sensitive or reactive pups. Solo pace, enrichment-led, and always followed by GPS + photo proof.

Dog Walking in Port Melbourne

Breezy foreshore strolls and wide residential streets make Port Melbourne perfect for relaxed, predictable walks. One-to-one means fewer triggers and a calmer dog at home afterwards.

Dog Walking in Middle Park

Quiet streets and pocket parks suit seniors and chill-tempered pups. We adjust distance and tempo for joints, weather and mood, prioritising sniffing and steady movement over speed.

Dog Walking in Southbank

For apartment dogs, structure is everything. We use riverside paths and quieter backstreets to keep arousal low and skills sharp — then send the GPS route and photos straight to your phone.
