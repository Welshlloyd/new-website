At The Woof of Walk Street, we believe every dog deserves a safe, steady, and personal walk — never part of a pack. Based in South Melbourne, our one-on-one dog walking is built for calmer outings, better manners, and real-world enrichment. You’ll get GPS routes, photo proof, and notes after each session.

Why choose solo dog walking?

Most dog walkers take 3–5 dogs at a time. We walk one dog only — every time for calmer, safer exercise and focused attention.

  • ✅ Personal pace & route (no pack pressure)
  • ✅ Confidence for anxious or reactive pups
  • ✅ Better manners reinforced
  • ✅ GPS route + photo updates after every walk

Dog Walking FAQs

Do you ever walk more than one dog at a time?
No — all walks are strictly solo. We never group unfamiliar dogs or use car-ferrying.
How long is each walk?
Each session is a full 45 minutes on foot, not including prep.
Do I get updates during the walk?
Yes — you’ll receive GPS tracking and photo updates after every walk.
