Our Favourite South Melbourne Walks

We love 45-minute loops that balance sniff time and steady pace. Here are a few of our favourites—great for weekday walks between 7am–7pm:

1) Albert Park Perimeter Loop

Flat paths, water views, and plenty of space. Ideal for focused striding with some sniff breaks.

2) South Melbourne Streets & Green Nooks

Quiet streets, pocket parks, and wide footpaths. Perfect for dogs who prefer consistent routines.

3) Bayside Breeze

A gentle there-and-back along the beach path. We check wind and heat, and keep paws off hot sand on warm days.
