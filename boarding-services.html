Boarding FAQs

Do you board multiple dogs at once?

No. We’re strictly one guest only. This keeps things calmer, safer, and more personal.

What’s included?

Multiple daily walks, GPS tracking, photo updates, drop off, food, chicken treats and calm home-style care. Bring any special diet if needed.

Can I book longer than a weekend?

Yes — choose a week (7 nights) or two weeks (14 nights) with built-in discounts.
