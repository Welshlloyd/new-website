Terms & Conditions

Last updated: February 2025

1. Services

We provide solo dog walking (45 minutes) and boutique weekend boarding, subject to availability and prior booking.

2. Membership Rules

Membership walks are available Monday to Friday, 7am–7pm, must be booked via the calendar, and are subject to availability. Unused credits do not roll over unless otherwise stated.

3. Casual Bookings

Casual bookings can be made via our booking system. Walks are non-refundable once confirmed.

4. Boarding

Boarding is strictly one dog at a time. Bookings require prior approval and confirmation. We reserve the right to refuse bookings if a dog shows signs of aggression or illness.

5. Payment

Payments are processed via secure third-party providers. Memberships are billed monthly. All services must be paid in advance.

6. Liability

While we take every precaution, The Woof of Walk Street is not liable for unforeseen accidents or illnesses. Owners must provide accurate information about their dog’s health and temperament.

7. Amendments

We may update these Terms from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
