Our Ethos

We started The Woof of Walk Street in South Melbourne with a simple belief: dogs do best with one-on-one attention. That’s why we only do solo walks and never waste time ferrying dogs in cars. Your dog’s 45 minutes are spent walking, sniffing, and decompressing.

What Makes Us Different

  • One dog at a time — safer, calmer, more focused
  • GPS tracking & photo updates after every walk
  • Boutique boarding — one guest only, like home (but quieter)
  • Transparent, ethical care and clear communication
