Terms of Service

Plain-English terms for a smooth experience.

Bookings & availability

All walks and boarding must be booked in our client portal calendar and are subject to availability. Walk windows are Mon–Fri, 7am–7pm.

One-dog-only policy

We never pack walk. Each service is delivered to one dog at a time, on-lead, and not car-ferried as “exercise”.

Cancellations & changes

You can change or cancel inside the portal within the displayed change window. Late cancellations may be charged in full.

Memberships

  • Starter: 4 solo walks/month – $295
  • Tail Growth: 10 solo walks/month – $750
  • Unlimited Pawfolio: weekdays, 1 walk/day (Mon–Fri) – $1,500

Unused walks don’t roll over. Unlimited covers one walk per weekday for the enrolled dog within the billing month.

Health & safety

Dogs must be microchipped, vaccinated as recommended by your vet, and free from contagious conditions. You authorise us to seek vet care in an emergency; you remain responsible for costs.

Keys & home access

If you provide keys or access codes, we’ll store them securely and use them only for your bookings.

Liability

We hold appropriate business insurance. To the extent permitted by law, our liability is limited to resupplying the service or refunding the amount paid for the affected booking.

Payments

Payments are processed via our client portal and payment providers. Prices include GST unless stated.

Changes

We may update these terms; the latest version is always on this page with an effective date.
