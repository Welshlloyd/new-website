Walking

How long are walks?

All walks are 45 minutes on foot. No car-ferrying and no group loops.

When are walks available?

Monday–Friday, 7am–7pm. All walks must be booked in the calendar and are subject to availability.

What’s included?

GPS tracking, photo updates, and notes after each walk so you know exactly where we went.

Memberships

We offer three plans: 4 walks (Starter Sniffvestment), 10 walks (Tail Growth Plan), and Unlimited (one weekday walk per day). Walks are Mon–Fri, 7am–7pm, booked via the calendar and subject to availability.

See Membership Plans

Boarding

Do you board multiple dogs?

No. We host one guest at a time for a calm, home-like stay.

Can you feed my dog?

Yes. We can feed your supplied food — or provide high-quality kibble and cooked chicken.
