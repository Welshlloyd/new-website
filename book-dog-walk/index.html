About Time To Pet

Why we use Time To Pet

  • Trusted globally: used by 4,000+ pet-care companies worldwide.
  • Bank-grade security: all portal communication uses 128-bit TLS encryption (same class as major banks).
  • Secure hosting: data is stored on hardened servers in data-centres with 24/7 physical security.
  • Resilient backups: customer data is backed up multiple times per day.
  • Everything in one place: booking requests, GPS walk maps, photo updates and messaging.
  • Privacy-first: we don’t store your card details on our site — it all lives securely in the portal.

What to expect

  1. Select an option and continue to the portal.
  2. Create your account and tell us about your dog.
  3. We confirm details (keys, routine) and lock your first walk.

Note: Time To Pet doesn’t support a separate “thank-you page”. Your confirmation appears in the portal and you’ll receive email updates.
