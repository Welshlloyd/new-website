Frequently Asked Questions

If it’s not answered here, ping us — we’re happy to help.

Walking

Do you ever walk more than one dog?

No. We are strictly one-dog-only. It’s safer, calmer, and delivers proper exercise and focus.

How long is a walk and what’s included?

Our casual walk is 45 minutes and includes GPS tracking, photo updates, and notes after each session.

When are walks available?

Walks are available Monday–Friday, 7:00am–7:00pm. All walks must be booked in the portal calendar and are subject to availability.

How much is a casual walk?

$80 per 45-minute walk (incl. GST). Frequent walkers usually save with a membership.

Memberships

What plans do you offer and pricing?
  • Starter Sniffvestment – 4 solo walks/month – $295
  • Tail Growth Plan – 10 solo walks/month – $750
  • Unlimited Pawfolio – Weekdays, 1 walk/day (Mon–Fri) – $1,500

All memberships follow the same rules: Mon–Fri 7am–7pm, calendar booking required, subject to availability, and unused walks don’t roll over.

Can I change times or skip a week?

You can move bookings inside our change window if a slot is available. Unused walks don’t roll over to the next month.

How does Unlimited work?

Unlimited allows one walk per weekday (Mon–Fri) for that dog in the membership month. It offers the biggest flexibility and best value if you walk frequently.

Weekend Woof Club (Boarding)

How many dogs stay at once?

One guest only. It’s calmer, cleaner, and feels like staying with family.

What do you feed?

We can feed your dog’s food or supply high-quality kibble and cooked chicken on request.

Do you support local shelters?

Yes. We donate 10% of profits to The Lost Dogs’ Home North Melbourne and other local dog charities.
