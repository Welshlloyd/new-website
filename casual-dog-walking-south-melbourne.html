Why Choose Our One-Dog-Only Casual Walks?

In South Melbourne and surrounding areas, many dog walking services group multiple dogs together. At The Woof of Walk Street, we specialise in one-dog-only casual walks. That means your dog gets focused attention, no pack chaos and a calm, relaxed outing each time.

Our walks run for a full 45 minutes on foot — not a quick stop or kennel pickup. We start from your home in South Melbourne (or nearby suburbs like Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park or Southbank) and take your dog on a gentle, personalised stroll through familiar local routes.

At the end of each walk you'll receive a GPS map and photo update so you can see exactly where your dog has been and how they enjoyed their outing.

What’s Included in the 45-Minute Solo Dog Walk

  • Full 45-minute uninterrupted walk — no multi-dog packs
  • Live GPS tracking + photo update via our portal
  • Local routes through South Melbourne’s favourite parks and streets
  • Positive, low-stress handling and focused attention
  • Fully insured & handler trained in pet first-aid
