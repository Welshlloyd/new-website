Best Dog Walks in South Melbourne

Our top picks for safe and scenic walks with your dog.

South Melbourne is home to some of the most dog-friendly walking routes in Melbourne. Whether you’re after a beachside stroll or a leafy park, here are our favourite spots.

1. South Melbourne Beach

A peaceful stretch with dog-friendly access and stunning city views.

2. Albert Park Lake

A classic Melbourne walk — plenty of open space and water views.

3. Gasworks Park

Great for social dogs and a local favourite for community vibes.

All our professional walks are solo, 45 minutes, GPS-tracked — your dog enjoys every minute.
