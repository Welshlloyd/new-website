FAQs — Solo Dog Walking & Boutique Dog Boarding (South Melbourne)

Real answers about our one-dog-only walks, Unlimited Membership, and one-guest-only boarding. If it’s not covered here, message us or call 0456 754 050.

Solo Dog Walking — South Melbourne

We specialise in focused, one-dog-only walks around South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park, and Southbank. Every walk includes GPS and photos in your secure portal.

Do you ever walk more than one dog?

No. We’re strictly one-dog-only. It’s safer, calmer, and delivers proper exercise and focus. See walking details →

How long is a casual walk and what’s included?

A casual walk is 45 minutes and includes GPS tracking, photo updates, and notes after each session.

When are walks available?

Walks are available Monday–Friday, 7:00am–7:00pm. All walks must be booked in the calendar and are subject to availability.

What does a casual walk cost?

$80 per 45-minute walk (incl. GST). Frequent walkers save with a membership. Compare memberships →

Dog Walking Memberships — Better Value

Memberships are designed for consistency and savings. You control bookings in the portal, and we keep you updated after every walk.

What plans do you offer and pricing?
  • Starter Sniffvestment — 4 solo walks/month — $300
  • Tail Growth Plan — 10 solo walks/month — $750
  • Unlimited Pawfolio — Weekdays, one walk/day (Mon–Fri) — $1,500

All memberships: Mon–Fri 7am–7pm, calendar booking required, subject to availability, unused walks don’t roll over. See Unlimited breakdown →

How does Unlimited work?

One walk per weekday for the membership month for that dog. Biggest flexibility + best value if you walk frequently. Learn more →

Can I change times or skip a week?

You can move bookings inside our change window if a slot is available. Unused walks don’t roll over.

Boutique Dog Boarding — One Guest Only

Our Weekend Woof Club offers one-guest-only, home-style boarding. It’s ideal for calm, predictable stays—more like visiting family than a kennel.

How many dogs stay at once?

One guest only. It’s calmer, cleaner, and feels like staying with family. Boarding overview →

Where does my dog sleep?

Home-style comfort: dogs can sleep on our bed or the sofa if that’s your home routine. We can also pick up your dog’s bed or crate locally before the stay.

What do you feed?

We can feed your dog’s food or supply high-quality kibble plus cooked chicken on request. Tell us about allergies in the portal.

Do you support local shelters?

Yes. We donate 10% of profits to local dog charities. See our impact →
