Quick Answers Before You Contact Us

Do you offer group/pack walks?

**Absolutely not.** We are 100% committed to **solo, one-dog-only walks** to ensure safety, reduce stress, and maximize learning and enrichment for your dog.

What is Woof Club Boarding?

It's our exclusive, **one-guest-only boutique boarding** service. Your dog enjoys a calm, home environment with dedicated attention, not a noisy kennel or a multi-dog household.

Yes. Our **Memberships** are designed for weekly consistency and include priority booking. New clients often qualify for an introductory discount on their first walk or boarding stay.
