Dog-Friendly Cafés in South Melbourne

Locals we love for good coffee, shade, and a warm welcome for dogs.

Post-walk coffee? Yes please. These are the spots we consistently find **dog-friendly** — outdoor seating, shade where possible, clean water bowls and staff who are happy to say hello. Always check current policies and be mindful of other guests.

Quick etiquette tips

  • Keep leads short; settle dogs beside or under the table on a small mat.
  • Skip peak times if your dog is still learning to relax in busy places.
  • Offer water and a chew to encourage calm resting.
  • Always thank staff — a friendly chat keeps cafés dog-welcoming.
