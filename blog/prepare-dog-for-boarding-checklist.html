Boarding Prep: A Handy Checklist

Set your dog up for a relaxed, enjoyable Weekend Woof Club stay.

A little prep goes a long way. Here’s what we recommend packing and sharing before your pup’s boutique stay.

What to pack

  • Regular food (or we can provide high-quality kibble/cooked chicken)
  • Lead, harness, and ID
  • Comfort item (bed blanket or worn t-shirt)
  • Medications with instructions
  • Vet details & emergency contact

Before drop-off

  • Keep the day calm — a short sniff-walk is perfect
  • Toilet stop just before drop-off
  • Share routine notes: wake times, feeding, quirks, training cues
