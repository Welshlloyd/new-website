About Time To Pet

Why we use Time To Pet

  • Secure portal: encrypted (HTTPS) for all logins and data.
  • Privacy-first: bookings and messages live in the portal; we don’t store your card on our site.
  • Everything in one place: booking requests, GPS walk maps, photo updates, messaging.
  • Trusted platform: used by pet-care businesses across Australia.

What to expect

  1. Select an option and continue to the portal.
  2. Create your account and tell us about your dog.
  3. We confirm details (keys, routine) and lock your first walk.

Note: Time To Pet doesn’t support a separate “thank-you page”. Your confirmation is shown inside the portal and you’ll receive email updates.
