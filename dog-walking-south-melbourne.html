Dog Walking FAQs

Do you offer group walks?

No — we do solo 45-minute walks only for calm, focused outings.

Which areas do you cover?

South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park, and Southbank.

Are walks GPS-tracked with photo updates?

Yes. Every walk includes GPS route tracking and a photo update.

Can you walk anxious or reactive dogs?

Yes — we’ll do a meet-and-greet and agree on a handling plan before the first walk.

How do I book?

Create an account or log in to our Time To Pet portal and select a 45-minute solo walk.
