Professional Dog Walking South Melbourne — One Dog at a Time

At The Woof of Walk Street, “solo” means solo — no pack walks, no car shuttles, no chaos. Your dog gets calm, focused attention from a dedicated walker who knows South Melbourne’s streets and parks. We keep routes familiar and enriching — think Albert Park Lake, St Vincent Gardens, and leafy backstreets — so your pup builds confidence, manners and a predictable routine.

Every session includes a live GPS route, a photo update, and a quick note about how your dog went. We use positive, force-free handling and adjust the pace for seniors, sniff-lovers, and high-energy pups alike. If you’re booking often, our dog walking memberships reduce your per-walk price and secure priority slots.

How Our Solo Dog Walks Work

  1. Book online: Choose a time between 7:00am–7:00pm (Mon–Fri) via our Time To Pet portal.
  2. We collect & walk: We start from your door and take a calm, one-on-one 45-minute walk — no group mixing.
  3. Proof & notes: You’ll receive a GPS route, photo and short summary straight after.

Dog Walking FAQs

Do you ever walk more than one dog at a time?

No — all walks are strictly solo. We never group unfamiliar dogs or use car-ferrying.

How long is each walk?

Each session is a full 45 minutes on foot, not including transport or prep.

Do I get updates after the walk?

Yes — you’ll receive a GPS route, a photo update, and quick notes after every walk.

Which areas do you cover?

South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park and Southbank.
