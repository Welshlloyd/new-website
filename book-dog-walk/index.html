About Time To Pet

Why we use Time To Pet

  • Trusted worldwide: used by 4,000+ pet-care companies.
  • Bank-level security: the site uses the same 128-bit encryption used by major banks.
  • Private by design: bookings, GPS maps, photo updates, and messages live in the portal — we don’t store your card on our site.
  • Secure hosting: data is kept on protected servers in monitored data centres with 24/7 security.
  • Safe backups: customer data is backed up multiple times a day.
  • Everything in one place: request walks, see confirmations, view GPS routes, and message us.

What to expect

Inside Time To Pet

  1. Create your account and tell us about your dog.
  2. Request your first walk or reply to our suggested time.
  3. See your booking confirmation in the portal.
  4. On walk day, view your GPS route and photo updates.

Note: Time To Pet doesn’t use a separate “thank-you page” — confirmations show in the portal and you’ll also get portal emails.
