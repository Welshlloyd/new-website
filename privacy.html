Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 Sep 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how The Woof of Walk Street (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses and protects your information when you use our website, booking portals, and services. We operate in Australia and handle personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information we collect

  • Client details: name, email, phone, suburb, emergency contact.
  • Dog details: name, breed, age, temperament, vaccination status, medical needs, vet clinic.
  • Booking & service info: requested dates/times, addresses for pick-up/drop-off, access instructions.
  • Media & tracking: walk photos and optional GPS routes we share with you as part of the service.
  • Website data: basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), cookies, device/browser info, and Instagram widget loads.
  • Payments & billing: processed by third parties (e.g., Time To Pet / your chosen processor). We do not store full card details.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and manage dog walking, memberships and boarding services.
  • To communicate about bookings, schedule changes, invoices and service updates.
  • To send GPS links and photos related to completed services.
  • To improve our website and services (analytics, troubleshooting, security).
  • With your consent, to feature anonymised photos or testimonials on our channels.

3. Third-party services

We use reputable providers to operate the business. Your data may be processed by:

  • Time To Pet (TTP) – client portal, scheduling, client profiles, messaging and billing.
  • Google Analytics – anonymous website usage metrics.
  • Instagram/SnapWidget – embedded gallery content may set their own cookies.
  • Email & cloud storage – for service communications and record-keeping.

Each provider has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. Where feasible, we enable privacy-respecting settings.

4. Cookies & analytics

We use essential cookies for site functionality and may use analytics cookies to understand aggregate traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site features (e.g., embedded Instagram feed).

5. Legal basis & disclosure

We collect personal information only where reasonably necessary for our functions. We may disclose information:

  • to deliver services you request (e.g., to TTP or emergency vet, if required);
  • to professional advisers (legal/insurance) where reasonably necessary;
  • if required by law, court order, or to protect safety and lawful interests.

6. Security & retention

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access or disclosure. We keep information only as long as needed for service, legal, and record-keeping requirements.

7. Access, correction & contact

You may request access to, or correction of, your personal information. To make a request or lodge a privacy complaint, contact:

Email: info@woofofwalkstreet.com
Phone: +61 4XX XXX XXX

We will respond within a reasonable time. If you are dissatisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

8. Children

Our services are directed to adults arranging pet care. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International transfers

Some providers may store or process information outside Australia. We take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients handle personal information in accordance with applicable privacy standards.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our practices or applicable laws. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
