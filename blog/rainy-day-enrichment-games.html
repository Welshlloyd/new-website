Rainy-Day Enrichment Games

Short, calm activities that take the edge off when the weather doesn’t play nice.

Exercise isn’t only about steps — mental work matters. Here are simple games we use with our walking clients when it’s too wet or windy for long outings.

Try these at home

  1. Towel Burrito: Roll treats in a towel and let your dog unroll to find them.
  2. Snuffle Scatter: Sprinkle kibble over a snuffle mat (or a grass patch).
  3. Muffin Tin Puzzle: Treats in a muffin tray, cover holes with tennis balls.
  4. Box Foraging: Cardboard box, paper scrunchies, a few treats to hunt.
  5. Calm Chew: A stuffed Kong or lick mat to lower arousal.
  6. Find It: Hide treats in one room, release with a cue to search.
  7. Decompression Walk: When the rain breaks, 15 minutes of sniffing time.

Solo walks with structured sniffing are part of our routine — we build calm into every outing.
