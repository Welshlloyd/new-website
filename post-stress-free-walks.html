How We Keep Every Walk Safe & Stress-Free

One dog at a time. That’s our starting point and our promise. We don’t pack-walk and we don’t ferry dogs in cars for most of the session. Your dog gets a full 45 minutes of real walking in their neighbourhood.

GPS Tracking & Photo Updates

After each walk, you’ll receive a GPS map and photo updates so you can see exactly where we went and how your dog enjoyed it.

Why It Matters

  • Lower stress: no group juggling, no van time.
  • Higher safety: 100% focus on your dog.
  • More exercise: every minute is for walking.
