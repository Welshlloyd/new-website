Boarding Prep: A Handy Checklist

Set your dog up for a relaxed, enjoyable Weekend Woof Club stay.

A little prep goes a long way. Here’s what we recommend packing and sharing before your pup’s boutique stay.

What to pack

  • Regular food (or note if you’d like our high-quality kibble/cooked chicken)
  • Lead, harness, and ID
  • Comfort item: bed blanket or a worn t-shirt
  • Medications with clear instructions
  • Vet details and emergency contact

Before drop-off

  • Keep the day calm — a short sniff-walk is perfect
  • Quick toileting stop just before drop-off
  • Share routine notes: wake times, feeding, quirks, training cues

We host one guest at a time, with daily updates and calm routines — just like home, only quieter.
