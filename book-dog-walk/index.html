About Time To Pet

Why we use Time To Pet

  • Secure portal: encrypted connection (HTTPS) for all logins and data.
  • Privacy-first: bookings and messages are managed in the portal; we don’t store your card details on our site.
  • Everything in one place: booking requests, GPS walk maps, photo updates and messaging.
  • Trusted by pet-care businesses: widely used across Australia and globally.

What to expect

  1. Select your option above and continue to the portal.
  2. Create your account and tell us about your dog.
  3. We confirm details (keys, routine) and lock your first walk.
