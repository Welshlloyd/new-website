About Us

Ethical dog walking and boarding in South Melbourne. One dog at a time, GPS tracked, with photo updates you can trust.

Our Story

The Woof of Walk Street was founded with a simple belief: dogs deserve individual attention, care, and respect. Too often, dog walkers load multiple dogs into cars or vans, ferrying them around with little actual walking time. We do things differently.

We provide one-dog-only, 45-minute walks with GPS tracking and photo updates so you know exactly where your dog has been, and how happy they are. Every booking means 100% of our focus is on your dog.
