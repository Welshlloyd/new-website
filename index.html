Solo Dog Walking in South Melbourne — GPS-Tracked & One-to-One

We don’t run pack walks or car shuttles. Your pup walks locally, one-on-one, at their own pace. After every walk you’ll receive GPS routes and photo updates. It’s the transparent, low-stress option anxious or reactive dogs love.

Our service area is centred on South Melbourne and includes Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park and Southbank. Walks run Mon–Fri, 7am–7pm, and weekend boarding is offered for one guest at a time.
