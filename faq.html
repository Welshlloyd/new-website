Frequently Asked Questions

If you don’t see your question here, send us a message.

Do you really walk only one dog at a time?

Always. We’re strictly one-dog-only. No pack walking, no car-ferrying from park to park. Your dog gets a full 45-minute walk and our complete attention.

What are your hours and service area?

Walks are available Mon–Fri, 7:00am–7:00pm within South Melbourne and nearby suburbs (Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park). Public holidays excluded.

How do I book a casual walk?

Use our client portal and choose your time: Book Casual in Time To Pet. You’ll create an account on first use.

How do membership credits work?

Members can request a walk using their credits here: Use Membership Credit. Credits apply to weekday, 45-minute solo walks. Unused walks don’t roll over unless specified in your plan.

What are the membership options?

Three plans: Starter (4 walks), Growth (10 walks), and Unlimited (one weekday walk/day). See details on the Memberships page.

Do you provide GPS tracking and photos?

Yes—every walk includes GPS tracking and a photo update so you can see where your dog went and how they’re doing.

How does Weekend Woof Club boarding work?

It’s a boutique, one-guest-only weekend stay. Choose Friday→Sunday or Friday→Monday. No public holiday surcharges. Photo updates included. Learn more on Boarding.

Can you feed my dog during boarding?

Absolutely. We can supply quality kibble and cooked chicken, or you can provide your own food. Let us know any preferences or allergies.

Are you insured and police-checked?

Yes. We’re fully insured and police-checked for your peace of mind.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Please cancel with as much notice as possible so we can offer the slot to another client. Late cancellations may forfeit a credit at our discretion during peak times.

We give back: 10% of profits are donated to The Lost Dogs’ Home (North Melbourne) and local shelters. Every booking helps a dog in need.
