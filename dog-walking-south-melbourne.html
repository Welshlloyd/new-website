Professional Dog Walkers in South Melbourne — Solo Only

At The Woof of Walk Street, “solo” means solo — no pack walks, no car shuttles, no chaos. Your dog gets calm, focused attention from an insured, police-checked, first-aid-trained walker who knows South Melbourne’s streets and parks. We favour familiar, enriching routes — think Albert Park Lake, St Vincent Gardens and leafy backstreets — so your pup builds confidence, manners and a predictable routine.

Every session includes a live GPS route, a photo update, and quick notes about how your dog went. Our force-free approach adapts to seniors, sniff-lovers and high-energy pups alike. If you’re booking often, our dog walking memberships reduce the per-walk price and secure priority slots (and our Unlimited plan is 50% off for the first month).

Looking for weekend care too? See our boutique Weekend Woof Club boarding — one guest at a time for a quiet, home-style stay.

How Our Dog Walking Service Works

  1. Book online: Choose a time between 7:00am–7:00pm (Mon–Fri) via our booking page.
  2. We collect & walk: We start from your door and take a calm, one-on-one 45-minute walk — never mixing unfamiliar dogs.
  3. Proof & notes: You’ll receive a GPS route, photo and short summary straight after.

Prefer a plan? Compare Starter (4), Growth (10) and Unlimited (weekdays). New to us? Read how we keep things ethical in The Ethical Dog Walker and why solo walks reduce stress.

Dog Walking FAQs

Do you ever walk more than one dog at a time?

No — all walks are strictly solo. We never group unfamiliar dogs or use car-ferrying.

How long is each walk?

Each session is a full 45 minutes on foot, not including transport or prep.

Do I get updates after the walk?

Yes — you’ll receive a GPS route, a photo update, and short notes after every walk.

Which areas do you cover?

South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park and Southbank.
