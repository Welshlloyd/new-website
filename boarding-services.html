Boarding FAQs

Do you board multiple dogs at once?

No. We’re strictly one guest only. This keeps things calmer, safer, and more personal.

Can my dog sleep on your bed or the sofa?

Yes — if that’s your home routine, we’ll continue it here. Comfort first.

What’s included?

Daily walks with GPS tracking, photo updates, food, enrichment games, and bones & chicken treats. Let us know about allergies.

Can you pick up our dog’s bed or crate?

Yes — for local clients we can collect and return beds or crates to keep your dog’s routine consistent.

Can I book for longer than a weekend?

Yes — custom stays can be arranged. Just reach out via our contact page.
