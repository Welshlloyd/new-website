Why Owners Choose Us

One-dog-only walks. One-guest-only boarding. We built The Woof of Walk Street for dogs who thrive on calm, individual attention. Your pup gets focused exercise, predictable routines and real home comfort — not a busy pack walk or a noisy kennel.

  • Walks include GPS route and photo updates—see exactly how their day went. Explore dog walking →
  • Boarding is home-style: dogs may sleep on our bed or the sofa if that’s your routine. See boarding details →
  • Frequent walkers save with memberships (including our Unlimited weekday option). Compare memberships →
  • We donate 10% of profits to local shelters. See our impact →

If you’re new here, the fastest way to get started is via our secure portal — it takes about a minute to create your account and request your first booking.
