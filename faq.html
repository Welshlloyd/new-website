Solo Dog Walking & Care: Core Questions

Our premium solo dog walking is built on safety and personalized care, ideal for sensitive or anxious dogs. We service South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, Middle Park, and Southbank. Find out why **one-dog-only** is better.

Do you ever walk more than one dog at the same time?

Absolutely not. We’re strictly **one-dog-only**. This guarantees focused attention, a calm experience, and ensures proper exercise for your dog, especially if they are sensitive or anxious. We never group walk. See walking details →

What's included in a casual walk?

A casual solo walk is **45 minutes** of dedicated one-on-one time, plus transport. Every walk includes **GPS tracking, photo updates, and detailed notes** delivered to your secure client portal.

When are the solo dog walks available?

Our standard hours are **Monday–Friday, 7:00am–7:00pm**. All walks must be booked through the portal calendar and are **subject to availability**. We recommend booking in advance.

What does a casual walk cost?

**$80** per 45-minute walk (incl. GST). Frequent walkers save significantly with a membership. Compare memberships →

Boutique Dog Boarding — Weekend Woof Club

Our boarding is strictly **one-guest-only**. We provide a calm, home-style experience that eliminates kennel stress and keeps your sensitive dog relaxed while you're away.

How many dogs stay at once for your boutique boarding service?

We are strictly **one-guest-only** for boarding. Your dog is the only guest, ensuring a calm, predictable, and clean home environment. It's more like staying with family. Boarding overview →

Where does my dog sleep during boarding?

**Home-style comfort:** we follow your routine. We are happy to welcome your dog to sleep on our bed or the sofa. We can also **pick up their familiar bed or crate** locally before the stay to maximize their comfort.

What do you feed during a boarding stay?

We prefer you supply your dog’s food to maintain consistency. Alternatively, we can supply high-quality kibble plus cooked chicken on request. Please detail any allergies or dietary needs in your client portal profile.

Do you support local shelters?

Yes. We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. We donate **10% of our profits** to local dog charities. See our impact →

Booking & Your Dog's Profile

We use the secure **Time To Pet** portal for all bookings and updates. This ensures your data is secure and all communication is centralized.

How do I book a service and manage my bookings?

All bookings and service management are handled through our secure online **Time To Pet Client Portal**. Once you create a profile, you can view real-time availability, schedule walks, confirm boarding dates, and see report cards.

Do I need to schedule a meet and greet?

We require a free, no-obligation meet and greet for all new boarding clients and recommend one for dog walking, especially for sensitive dogs. This ensures a comfortable first experience for your dog.
