At The Woof of Walk Street, every outing is one-on-one — never a pack. Based in South Melbourne, our calm, enrichment-focused walks help with manners and decompression. You’ll get the GPS route, photos and quick notes after each session.

Why choose solo dog walking?

Most walkers take 3–5 dogs at once. We walk one dog only — every time for safer, calmer, more focused exercise tailored to your pup.

  • Calmer, more controlled walks (great for anxious or reactive pups)
  • Personalised pace and route — no rushing with a pack
  • Real-world manners reinforced gently
  • GPS route + photo proof after every walk

Dog Walking FAQs

Do you ever walk more than one dog at a time?
No — all walks are strictly solo. We never group unfamiliar dogs or use car-ferrying.
How long is each walk?
Each session is a full 45 minutes on foot, not including transport or prep.
Do I get updates during the walk?
Yes — GPS tracking and photo updates after every walk.
